# 🪨📄✂️ Rock Paper Scissors

> **Think you're lucky? Prove it.A classic game. A modern look. One click to decide your fate.** 🎮

## ✨ About the Project

Challenge the computer in the timeless **Rock Paper Scissors** game! Pick your move, let the computer make its choice, and see who comes out on top. Every click updates the score instantly and keeps the battle going.

Simple. Fast. Fun.

---

## 🚀 Features

🎯 Interactive gameplay with a single click
🤖 Random computer move generation
🏆 Live score tracking for both player and computer
🎨 Dynamic result messages with color feedback
📱 Clean, responsive, and beginner-friendly interface

---

## 🛠️ Built With

* **HTML5**
* **CSS3**
* **JavaScript**

---

## 🎮 How to Play

1. Choose **Rock**, **Paper**, or **Scissors**.
2. The computer randomly selects its move.
3. The winner is decided instantly.
4. Scores update automatically after every round.
5. Keep playing and try to beat the computer!
